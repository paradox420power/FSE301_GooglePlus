We don't have a cool story. We aren't the next Steve Jobs, Bill Gates, or Tom (from Myspace). We're just a bunch of extra normal undergraduate students at Arizona State University that have a problem of getting sucked into the black hole or interweb. We do hope to eventually be rich. Who wouldn't? But it probably will not be through this screen time management tool. Instead, we simply hope that this tool will help you and I steal back a few minutes of our precious time, the only commodity we can't get back, and free us up to help make a difference, to do good, and to live.
